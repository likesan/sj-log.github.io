    C language, TIL

    i += j += k;

    This assignment seems tricky. but Knking’s book say, there is a tip.

    i += (j += k);

    so, j+=k becomes (j=j+k), hence operated first. at here, K never has any operating without only its initialization. if k = 3; k becomes just 3.

    j becomes j+3.

    and,

    i = i+ (j+3).
