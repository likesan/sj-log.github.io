    How to Express Emoji in Custom Hugo

    March 23, 2021
    Hugo, Emoji

    It’s easy. #

    1. Setting enableEmoji=true into config.toml in root directory(not in theme’s config.toml file)
    2. add the parent tag where you want to express emoji, as the following below <body {{ emojify 1}}>
